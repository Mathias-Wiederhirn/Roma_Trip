Tania, Eleni & Mat's Rome Trip

Base: Trianon Borgo Pio Aparthotel, Piazza delle Vaschette 13, Vaticano Prati, Rome

Trip dates: Wednesday 15 April 2026 to Sunday 19 April 2026.

Wednesday 15 April 2026 | Arrival + Easy First Evening

Goal: gentle arrival, no pressure, and a first evening focused on the Prati neighborhood close to the hotel.

  • 16:40 Arrive at Rome airport. If this is Fiumicino (FCO), a licensed white city taxi to the hotel area is usually the easiest choice for 3 people with luggage. Allow roughly 45 to 60 minutes to reach Prati depending on traffic.
  • 18:00 Expected hotel arrival, check-in, and a short refresh break.
  • 19:00 Leave hotel for an easy walk into the Prati neighborhood and around Via Cola di Rienzo. 🚶 Walk 10 to 15 min
  • 19:15 Prati stroll for your first Rome atmosphere, shopping streets, and relaxed neighborhood feel.
  • 20:15 Dinner at New Food Experience, a fully gluten-free Prati restaurant close to the hotel.
  • 21:30 Slow walk back to the hotel. 🚶 Walk 8 to 10 min

Thursday 16 April 2026 | Vatican Museums, Hotel Rest, Castel Sant'Angelo

Goal: keep the museum morning efficient, lunch close to the Vatican Museums, rest at the hotel, then enjoy Castel Sant'Angelo in the afternoon and evening.

  • 10:30 Leave hotel and walk to the Vatican meeting point. 🚶 Walk about 15 min
  • 10:45 Meeting point: Via Vespasiano, 28, 00192 Roma.
  • 11:00 Booked guided tour: Vatican Museums + Sistine Chapel.
  • 14:10 Lunch close to the museum at New Food Experience. 🚶 Walk about 10 min
  • 15:15 Walk back to the hotel. 🚶 Walk about 20 min
  • 15:35 Hotel rest break for 2 hours.
  • 17:40 Leave hotel for Castel Sant'Angelo. 🚶 Walk about 12 min
  • 18:00 Castle and bridge area for the afternoon and evening atmosphere, including Ponte Sant'Angelo.
  • 19:45 Dinner at La Soffitta Renovatio, a gluten-free-friendly classic close to the Vatican.
  • 21:00 Back at hotel.

Friday 17 April 2026 | Capitoline Hill, Monti, Colosseum Underground

Goal: spend the day on the Ancient Rome side and arrive at the Colosseum without stress.

  • 10:30 Leave hotel and take a taxi to Piazza Venezia. 🚕 Taxi about 15 to 20 min
  • 10:55 Explore Piazza Venezia and take in the big Roman overview from the outside of the Vittoriano.
  • 11:20 Walk up to Capitoline Hill for one of the best overviews toward the Forum. 🚶 Walk about 8 min
  • 11:50 Walk toward the charming Monti neighborhood. 🚶 Walk about 15 min
  • 12:05 Early lunch in Monti at Pasta in Corso Monti-Colosseo, a dedicated gluten-free option.
  • 13:05 Walk from Monti to the Colosseum meeting point. 🚶 Walk about 12 to 15 min
  • 13:20 Meeting point: Via del Colosseo, 31, 00184 Roma.
  • 13:40 Booked guided tour: Colosseum with underground visit.
  • 15:45 Short pause for drinks or gelato near the Colosseum.
  • 16:15 Walk along Via dei Fori Imperiali for the grand views back toward Piazza Venezia. 🚶 Walk about 18 min
  • 16:45 Optional stop at the Forum overlook or stay in Monti for a slower neighborhood walk.
  • 18:30 Dinner in Monti at Pasta in Corso Monti-Colosseo.
  • 20:00 Taxi back to the hotel. 🚕 Taxi about 15 to 20 min

Saturday 18 April 2026 | Piazza Navona, Pantheon, Trevi, Spanish Steps, Trastevere

Goal: classic historic-center highlights in one flow, then a relaxed afternoon and dinner in Trastevere.

  • 10:30 Leave hotel and walk to Piazza Navona. 🚶 Walk about 18 to 20 min
  • 10:50 Piazza Navona stroll and fountain photos.
  • 11:20 Walk to the Pantheon. 🚶 Walk about 8 min
  • 11:30 Visit the Pantheon. A shorter stop works well here. Allow queue time if needed.
  • 12:05 Coffee break nearby.
  • 12:25 Walk to the Trevi Fountain. 🚶 Walk about 10 min
  • 12:35 Trevi Fountain stop and photos.
  • 12:55 Walk to the Spanish Steps. 🚶 Walk about 10 to 12 min
  • 13:10 Spanish Steps and the upper terrace viewpoint.
  • 13:45 Lunch near the Pantheon at Risotteria Melotti, an all-gluten-free lunch option near Piazza Navona.
  • 15:15 Return to the hotel for an afternoon break. 🚕 Taxi about 15 min
  • 17:30 Taxi to Trastevere. 🚕 Taxi about 15 min
  • 17:50 Explore the lanes around Santa Maria in Trastevere, ideal for a teenager-friendly slower hour with snacks, shops, and street life.
  • 18:30 Gelato or aperitivo in Trastevere.
  • 19:30 Dinner in Trastevere at Mama Eat Roma Trastevere.
  • 21:00 Taxi back to the hotel. 🚕 Taxi about 15 min

Sunday 19 April 2026 | Check-out Day, Castle Visit, Easy Final Hours

Goal: stay close to the hotel, keep luggage simple, and leave enough margin before the evening flight.

  • 09:30 Check out or settle luggage storage with the hotel before leaving.
  • 10:30 Walk to Castel Sant'Angelo. 🚶 Walk about 10 min
  • 10:45 Visit Castel Sant'Angelo if you would like one last major monument from the inside. Otherwise, keep it as an exterior and bridge photo stop.
  • 12:15 Walk to Campo de' Fiori for lunch at Pasta in Corso Vittorio Emanuele. 🚶 Walk about 15 min
  • 13:30 Gentle stroll back via Piazza Navona and the river, or simply relax in Prati for shopping and coffee. 🚶 Walk 20 to 25 min
  • 16:00 Final coffee break close to the hotel and collect luggage.
  • 17:00 Early light dinner or aperitivo in Prati at Mama Eat Roma Vaticano if you would like one last relaxed gluten-free meal before the airport.
  • 18:15 Leave hotel for the airport. For a 21:25 flight, this is a comfortable taxi departure time for Fiumicino. If your airport is Ciampino, I would leave a little earlier if traffic looks heavy.
  • 21:25 Departure flight.
