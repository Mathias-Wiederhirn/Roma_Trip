Tania, Eleni & Mat's Rome Trip

Base: Piazza delle Vaschette 13, Vaticano Prati, Rome

Trip dates: Wednesday 15 April 2026 to Sunday 19 April 2026.

Wednesday 15 April 2026 | Arrival + Easy First Evening

Goal: gentle arrival, no pressure, stay close to the hotel and enjoy the Vatican riverside area.

  • 16:40 Arrive at Rome airport. If this is Fiumicino (FCO), a licensed white city taxi to the hotel area is usually the easiest choice for 3 people with luggage. Allow roughly 45 to 60 minutes to reach Prati depending on traffic.
  • 18:00 Expected hotel arrival, check-in, and a short refresh break.
  • 19:00 Leave hotel for an easy walk through Borgo Pio and toward St Peter's Square. Walk 8 to 10 min
  • 19:15 St Peter's Square and Via della Conciliazione for your first Rome views, photos, and evening atmosphere.
  • 20:00 Dinner in the Borgo Pio area. This keeps the evening relaxed and very close to the hotel.
  • 21:30 Slow walk back to the hotel. Walk 8 to 10 min

Thursday 16 April 2026 | Vatican Museums, St Peter's, Castel Sant'Angelo

Goal: keep the entire day on the Vatican side so there is very little backtracking.

  • 10:30 Leave hotel and walk toward the Vatican Museums area. Walk about 18 min
  • 10:50 Arrive early for the meeting point, water break, tickets, and security buffer.
  • 11:00 Booked guided tour: Vatican Museums + Sistine Chapel. Keep energy for this long indoor visit.
  • 14:10 Lunch near the museum exit or back in Prati. Walk 10 to 15 min
  • 15:20 Coffee break in Prati or Borgo Pio.
  • 15:45 Walk to St Peter's Square and enjoy the exterior and atmosphere. Walk about 15 min
  • 16:15 Optional entry to St Peter's Basilica only if the queue looks reasonable. If the line is long, keep this as an exterior stop and enjoy the square instead.
  • 17:10 Walk to Castel Sant'Angelo and Ponte Sant'Angelo. Walk about 14 min
  • 17:25 Riverside walk, angel bridge views, and photo stop at sunset light.
  • 18:15 Return toward Borgo Pio / Prati for aperitivo. Walk about 12 min
  • 19:15 Dinner near the hotel so the day ends easily after the museum tour.
  • 21:00 Back at hotel.

Friday 17 April 2026 | Capitoline Hill, Monti, Colosseum Underground

Goal: spend the day on the Ancient Rome side and arrive at the Colosseum without stress.

  • 10:30 Leave hotel and take a taxi to Piazza Venezia or Capitoline Hill. Taxi about 15 to 20 min
  • 10:55 Explore the Piazza Venezia area and take in the big Roman overview from the outside of the Vittoriano.
  • 11:20 Walk up to Capitoline Hill for one of the best overviews toward the Forum. Walk about 8 min
  • 11:50 Walk toward the charming Monti neighborhood. Walk about 15 min
  • 12:05 Early lunch in Monti so you are comfortable before the underground visit.
  • 13:10 Walk from Monti to the Colosseum meeting point. Walk about 12 to 15 min
  • 13:25 Arrive early, use restrooms, and keep passports or ID ready if your ticket requires it.
  • 13:40 Booked guided tour: Colosseum with underground visit.
  • 15:45 Short pause for drinks or gelato near the Colosseum.
  • 16:15 Walk along Via dei Fori Imperiali for the grand views back toward Piazza Venezia. Walk about 18 min
  • 16:45 Optional stop at the Forum overlook or stay in Monti for a slower neighborhood walk.
  • 18:00 Dinner in Monti, a lovely area with a local feel and easy family-friendly options.
  • 20:00 Taxi back to the hotel. Taxi about 15 to 20 min

Saturday 18 April 2026 | Piazza Navona, Pantheon, Trevi, Spanish Steps, Trastevere

Goal: classic historic-center highlights in one flow, then a relaxed afternoon and dinner in Trastevere.

  • 10:30 Leave hotel and walk to Piazza Navona. Walk about 18 to 20 min
  • 10:50 Piazza Navona stroll and fountain photos.
  • 11:20 Walk to the Pantheon. Walk about 8 min
  • 11:30 Visit the Pantheon. A shorter stop works well here. Allow queue time if needed.
  • 12:05 Coffee break nearby.
  • 12:25 Walk to the Trevi Fountain. Walk about 10 min
  • 12:35 Trevi Fountain stop and photos.
  • 12:55 Walk to the Spanish Steps. Walk about 10 to 12 min
  • 13:10 Spanish Steps and the upper terrace viewpoint.
  • 13:45 Lunch around Via della Croce / Campo Marzio.
  • 15:00 Taxi to the Janiculum Terrace for a panoramic Rome view. Taxi about 15 min
  • 15:20 Janiculum viewpoint and relaxing break.
  • 16:00 Walk down into Trastevere. Walk about 15 min downhill
  • 16:20 Explore the lanes around Santa Maria in Trastevere, ideal for a teenager-friendly slower hour with snacks, shops, and street life.
  • 18:00 Gelato or aperitivo in Trastevere.
  • 19:15 Dinner in Trastevere.
  • 21:00 Taxi back to the hotel. Taxi about 15 min

Sunday 19 April 2026 | Check-out Day, Castle Visit, Easy Final Hours

Goal: stay close to the hotel, keep luggage simple, and leave enough margin before the evening flight.

  • 09:30 Check out or settle luggage storage with the hotel before leaving.
  • 10:30 Walk to Castel Sant'Angelo. Walk about 10 min
  • 10:45 Visit Castel Sant'Angelo if you would like one last major monument from the inside. Otherwise, keep it as an exterior and bridge photo stop.
  • 12:15 Walk to Campo de' Fiori or the nearby lanes for lunch. Walk about 15 min
  • 13:30 Gentle stroll back via Piazza Navona and the river, or simply relax in Prati for shopping and coffee. Walk 20 to 25 min total if using Piazza Navona route
  • 16:00 Final coffee break close to the hotel and collect luggage.
  • 18:15 Leave hotel for the airport. For a 21:25 flight, this is a comfortable taxi departure time for Fiumicino. If your airport is Ciampino, I would leave a little earlier if traffic looks heavy.
  • 21:25 Departure flight.
